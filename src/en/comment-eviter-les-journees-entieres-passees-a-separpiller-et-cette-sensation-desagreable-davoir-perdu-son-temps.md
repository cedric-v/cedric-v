      <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">Here are the only <strong>2 productivity tips</strong> I've kept over the years and use on a daily basis. Two sure-fire ways of keeping the focus on what's essential, without piling on the "little things".</p>
      
      <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">(I'll cut to the chase when it comes to clarifying what calls you to an activity that makes sense to you. This will be the subject of another publication).</p>
      
      <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">You've clearly identified what you need to do. Now it's a matter of making sure you stay focused and keep moving in that direction every day.</p>
      
      <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">Here are <strong>2 tips</strong> to make sure you do:</p>
      
      <div class="space-y-6">
        <div>
          <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#0f172a] mb-3"><strong>1)</strong> The 3 things method</h3>
          <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed mb-2">At the end of each day, identify and write down the <strong>3 most important things</strong> to do on your next working day. When the time comes, do them. Well done. üëè</p>
          <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed mb-2"><strong>The rest is a bonus.</strong> üéâ</p>
          <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">If you then try new things and spread yourself too thin, that's OK. If you have urgent things to do, that's OK. If you're interrupted, it's OK. <strong>You've already done what you needed to do to make your working day a success.</strong> Do it again for the next day.</p>
        </div>
        
        <div>
          <h3 class="text-xl font-semibold text-[#0f172a] mb-3"><strong>2)</strong> The 2-minute rule</h3>
          <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed">If an action takes <strong>less than 2 minutes</strong> to complete, do it immediately. Don't write it down: writing it down and coming back to it later will take you longer than doing it right away.</p>
          <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed text-[#1f1f1f]/70 italic">(This is a tip from the GTD method - Getting Things Done).</p>
        </div>
      </div>
      
      <p class="text-lg leading-relaxed mt-6">Ready to try out these 2 tips combined for a few weeks and see the changes? What are your own tips for staying the course and moving forward each day without spreading yourself too thin?</p>
